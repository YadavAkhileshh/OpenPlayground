How to Play

  • The maze is visible briefly at the start.
  • After preview, walls disappear.
  • Use Arrow keys or WASD to move.
  • Hitting walls may reset you based on difficulty.
  • Reach the exit to win.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript only. No frameworks. No shortcuts.
