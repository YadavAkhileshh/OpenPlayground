Introvert

Definition: Prefers calm environments and deep conversations.

Traits: Reflective, thoughtful, independent.

Strengths: Good listener, focused, creative.

Fun Fact: Introverts recharge by spending time alone.

Extrovert

Definition: Gains energy from social interactions.

Traits: Outgoing, expressive, energetic.

Strengths: Great communicator, confident, enthusiastic.

Fun Fact: Extroverts recharge by being around people.

Ambivert

Definition: Balanced between introvert and extrovert traits.

Traits: Flexible, adaptable, balanced.

Strengths: Can work alone or in teams easily.

Fun Fact: Most people fall somewhere in the middle.
