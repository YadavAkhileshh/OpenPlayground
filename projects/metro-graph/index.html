📖 Graph Routing Manual

The Mathematics:

This engine relies on two major computer science concepts:

  • Force-Directed Spacing: When new stations spawn, they use repulsion mathematics to push away from existing stations, keeping the map organic and readable.
  • Graph Theory & Routing: The subway lines act as edges, and stations act as nodes. AI passengers use network logic to board trains heading toward their desired shape.

How to Play:

  1. Click and drag from one station to another to create a subway line.
  2. You have a maximum of 3 colored lines available.
  3. Small shapes next to a station are waiting passengers. A square passenger wants to go to a square station.
  4. Trains spawn automatically on your lines. If a station gets too crowded, the system overloads and the simulation ends!

💥 System Overload

A station became too crowded and the transit network collapsed.
