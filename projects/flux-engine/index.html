📖 Fluid Dynamics Manual

The Mathematics (Navier-Stokes):

This engine solves for fluid motion across a 100x100 grid using three main mathematical steps every single frame:

  • Advection: Moving the fluid through itself based on the velocity vector field.
  • Diffusion: Spreading the dye out over time using a Gauss-Seidel relaxation algorithm.
  • Projection: Forcing the fluid to be incompressible ($\nabla \cdot u = 0$) so it swirls in realistic vortexes instead of collapsing.

How to Interact:

  1. Drop Dye: Click and drag to inject colored dye and velocity vectors into the fluid.
  2. Draw Walls: Switch modes and draw solid boundaries. Watch how the fluid mathematically calculates pressure to flow *around* the obstacles!
