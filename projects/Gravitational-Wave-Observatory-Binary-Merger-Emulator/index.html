Gravitational Waves

Gravitational waves are ripples in spacetime caused by accelerating masses. Binary mergers of black holes or neutron stars produce detectable waves.

  • Inspiral: Orbits decay due to energy loss
  • Merger: Objects combine into one
  • Ringdown: Final settling to equilibrium
