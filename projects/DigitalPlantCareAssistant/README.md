# Digital Plant Care Assistant

A simple web app to help users log their real plants, get watering/fertilizing reminders, and track plant health.

## Features
- Log your plants (name & type)
- Get reminders for watering and fertilizing
- Track plant health status over time
- Data saved in your browser (localStorage)

## Usage
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Add your plants and start tracking!
