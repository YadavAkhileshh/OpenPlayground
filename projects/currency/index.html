Intaglio Printing

The portrait of Mahatma Gandhi, the Ashoka Pillar emblem, and identification marks (bleed lines) are printed in raised ink. You can feel these features by running your finger over the note.

Tactile Feature

Watermark & Window

The Mahatma Gandhi portrait and electrotype watermark (denominational numeral) appear when the note is held against light. The windowed security thread changes from green to blue on tilting.
