Cyber Safety Guide

Learn how to protect yourself online with simple and practical cybersecurity tips.

Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information through fake emails or websites.

  • Check sender email carefully
  • Do not click suspicious links
  • Verify website URL

Strong Passwords

Use complex passwords to protect your accounts from hackers.

  • Use at least 12 characters
  • Include symbols & numbers
  • Avoid personal information

Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security beyond just your password.

  • Enable OTP verification
  • Use authenticator apps
  • Never share verification codes

Social Media Privacy

Protect your personal data shared on social platforms.

  • Set profile to private
  • Limit personal details
  • Review privacy settings regularly

Public WiFi Risks

Public networks can expose your sensitive data.

  • Avoid banking on public WiFi
  • Use VPN services
  • Disable file sharing

Malware & Viruses

Malicious software designed to damage or steal data.

  • Install antivirus software
  • Keep software updated
  • Download only from trusted sites
